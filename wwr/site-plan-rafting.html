Splash White Water Rafting Site Plan

Traek Malan

Overview

Purpose

We need an online presence to engage past customers through content sharing and review posts and create a place for potential new clients to book one of our experience packages. The site will educate web visitors on how difficulty is rated in different river sections, how to prepare for a white water rafting experience, and learn how others have rated their own experiences with our company.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our primary target customers are young, physically active young adults, ages 14-25. Our secondary customers will be organizations bringing some or all of their members together for one or more organizational goals.

What do they want? The primary customers are looking for adventure and to conquer challenging situations, symbolic of how they wish to engage life: head on. Secondary customers are organizations seeking to improve communication, foster bonding opportunities, and create memorable experiences for continued group cohesion.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Individual clients and organizations will find detailed information on what experiences are available that meet their needs, including trip planning, river traversal options, guide availability, programs, packages, packing lists, and put-in/take-out spots corresponding with their selections. They will also find reviews, pictures, and detailed itineraries of standard and custom packages that previous clients have submitted, to gain an idea of what to expect both in the experience and how it affected them afterward.
