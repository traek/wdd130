Traek Malan | WDD 130

Hi, my name is Traek Malan (pronounced TRAYK MAY-len). I have seven children and we have one grandchild, so far. We have lived in the United States in Arizona, in a big small town named Maricopa, for 15 years. We love our neighbors, including the Gila River Nation to the immediate North and the Ak-Chin Nation to the immediate South. I volunteer in my community teaching kids 7-17 to approach technology as a creator rather than as a consumer through tinkering, programming, and various "making" skills. I currently serve as our ward's Temple and Family History Leader and love spending time in the FamilySearch Center helping community members research their family histories.

  • Manti Utah Temple - where my wife and I received our endowments
  • Bountiful Utah Temple - where my wife and I were sealed
  • Jordan River Temple - where we received special revelation about our children
  • Gilbert Arizona Temple - where I served as an ordinance worker
  • Layton Utah Temple - not yet dedicated, near our first home

Arizona

Arizona is located in the Southwestern United States, famous for the location of the Grand Canyon. Though largely desert, Arizona is home to diverse environments and supports year-round outdoor activities from golf or skiing to river rafting and spelunking.
